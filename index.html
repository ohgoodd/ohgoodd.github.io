Spencer Dent

Spencer's Biography

Spencer Dent is a London-born, Glasgow-based multimedia artist. They studied Fine Art Photography at Glasgow School of Art, graduating in 2022. Their work focuses the topic of ‘Genderlessness’ and ambiguous shape and form. They employ a variety of mediums to explore this topic including photography, printmaking, weaving, sculpture and painting. Their practice is focused on how shape and form can be distorted for the viewer, creating visuals that read as intangible or even confusing.
